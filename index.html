Goals of the Network

Improve Diagnostic Accuracy

Reduce false positives and negatives by exploring new biomarkers and technologies for earlier, more reliable disease detection

Improve Treatment Outcomes

Develop personalized treatment plans and predictive models to improve patient outcomes and reduce healthcare costs

Evaluate Patient Quality of Life

Measure and improve patient quality of life through objective assessments and patient-reported outcomes

Identify Risk Factors

Identify and address risk factors for disease progression and improve patient outcomes

Optimize Healthcare Delivery and Policy

Optimize healthcare delivery and policy through data-driven insights and evidence-based recommendations

Promote Health Equity

Address health disparities and improve access to care for underserved populations
