PHASES

Promoting Health Aging through Semantic Enrichment of Solitude Research - U01AG088074


Principal Investigators

John Beverly and Bill Duncan

Overview

Past research on solitude highlights negative outcomes, e.g. mortality or depression, of spending time alone. Research on gerotranscendence focuses on psychological connectedness beyond oneself in aging, emphasizing healthy aging and positive outcomes such as enhanced psychological well-being. PHASES aims to bridge the gap in both research areas and develop the Solitude Ontology and Gerotranscendence Ontology.

Aims

1.

Develop ontologies for solitude, gerotranscendence, and healthy aging research

2.

Create an open-access ontology based web interface integrating solitude, gerotranscendence, and healthy aging data.

3.

Promote our ontologies, train stakeholders to investigate the impact of solitude on successful aging and disseminate results to the broader community.
